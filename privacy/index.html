What is Bitcookies?

Bitcookies is my development brand and is used on my Windows and macOS software & expansion.

About Privacy

Bitcookies websites do not actively collect information from users, but only passively in some cases. Related information is as follows:

1. Cookies

We currently do not have any interactive features and do not generate user cookies.

2. Local Storage

Local Storage stores some of your settings, such as selected theme information, so that the next time you visit, you can use the previous settings directly.

You can also click the button below to delete Local Storage directly.

3. Service Workers

Service Workers will store some of the site's static resources to make the next visit quicker.

You can also click the button below to unregister Service Workers directly.

Software & Expansion Privacy

Due to the different features of each software and expansion, the privacy policy will be different. Therefore, the privacy statements of software and expansions will be stated separately on each software and expansion page. We will not write it out in detail here.
